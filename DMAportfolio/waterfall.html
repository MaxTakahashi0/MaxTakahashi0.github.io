Waterfall

This is my second example of a test of technology through audio and a bit of video. In this piece, I created a narrative of someone walking through a forest, lost, when they hear a waterfall in the distance. They walk closer and as they do the noise gets louder, however the closer they get the more it sounds like static rather than running water.

The way I achieved this affect is by taking the source code of an image of a waterfall I took, and uploading it into Audacity. I edited it slightly, but used as close to the direct output as I could. Afterwards, I edited some stock sounds together in Premiere Pro and was able to make a more immersive auditory experience. Enjoy!
