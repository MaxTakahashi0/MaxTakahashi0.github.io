Abstract Video Project

This was an experimental video project reflecting on how basic sounds and visuals can affect us, especially when percieved through a video.

For example, a brush on a piece of paper could be soothing for one person and grating to another. Not only does it depend on the audio systems of each individual, but it is also simply a matter of personal perspective.
